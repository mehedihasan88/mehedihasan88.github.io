About

Mehedi Hasan | Software Engineer, Samsung RnD Bangladesh

I’m Mehedi Hasan, a Software Engineer at Samsung R&D Institute Bangladesh. I earned my B.Sc. in Computer Science & Engineering from Jahangirnagar University, sharpening my skills through competitive programming. At Samsung, I develop AI-driven features like liquid consumption estimation and image-to-contact conversion, and engineer backend systems to manage Nowbar items on Watch 8. I’ve built a link preview PoC for Watch 7 notifications, analyzed its security across development approaches, and explored remote view integration with custom graphics for watchfaces. I also worked on integrating Watch 8’s Nowbar with Google’s AOD interface. I’m passionate about ML, Image Processing, and intelligent systems.
